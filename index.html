Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Clinical Study Protocol: This application is a clinical tool. Data collected is used for research purposes under specific institutional protocols.

1. Information Collection

Usage Data: We collect non-personally identifiable information (non-PII) such as device type and feature usage to improve app stability.

Personal Information: We require an email address for clinical users (physicians/nurses) to sync data. Optional PII like name and phone number may be provided.

Patient Pseudonymization: We do not collect Patient PII. Unique codes are used to identify participants; only the authorized clinical user (the physician) can link these codes back to a specific individual.

2. Data Hosting & Security

Infrastructure: All data is securely hosted on Google Cloud Platform (GCP). We utilize enterprise-grade encryption at rest and in transit.

Data Usage: VocaVibe does not sell or rent information to third parties. Data is used strictly for the clinical services provided by the app.

3. Account Deletion & Study Withdrawal

Because this app is an instrument of a clinical study, users who wish to stop participating must formally notify their Study Coordinator or contact us at info@vocavibe.ai.

Deleting the app from your device does not automatically remove your data from the research database. Formal withdrawal is required to ensure compliance with study ethics and data integrity requirements.

4. Safeguards

Access to any identifiable information is restricted to authorized personnel only, following strict security protocols within our GCP environment.
